# Future Games for Solana PvP Arena

## Pick Higher (Current Game)

Players pick tiles with hidden values, highest total score wins.

- **Modes:** 1x3, 3x9, 5x16
- **Team Sizes:** 1v1, 2v2, 5v5, 1v10, 2v20, 4v40

---

## Plinko

Drop balls through pegs to score points based on landing slots. Higher risk slots offer bigger multipliers.

- **Modes:** 3 balls/3 levels, 5 balls/5 levels, 10 balls/8 levels
- **Team Sizes:** 1v1, 2v2, 5v5, 1v10, 2v20, 4v40

## Dice

Roll dice and sum the highest values to win. More dice = more variance.

- **Modes:** 2 dice, 4 dice, 6 dice
- **Team Sizes:** 1v1, 2v2, 5v5, 1v10, 2v20, 4v40

## Coin Flip

Classic heads or tails prediction with best-of series options.

- **Modes:** Best of 1, Best of 3, Best of 5
- **Team Sizes:** 1v1, 2v2, 5v5

## Minesweeper

Find safe tiles without hitting mines. Bigger grids = higher stakes.

- **Modes:** 3x3 (2 mines), 5x5 (5 mines), 8x8 (10 mines)
- **Team Sizes:** 1v1, 2v2, 5v5

## Wheel Spin

Spin a fortune wheel with multiplier segments. Land on the highest multiplier to win.

- **Modes:** 8 segments, 16 segments, 32 segments
- **Team Sizes:** 1v1, 2v2, 5v5, 1v10

## Blackjack

Classic card game - get closest to 21 without busting. Players compete for best hand.

- **Modes:** 1 deck, 2 decks, 4 decks
- **Team Sizes:** 1v1, 2v2, 5v5

## Rock Paper Scissors

Fast-paced RPS with bonus rounds and power-ups.

- **Modes:** Best of 3, Best of 5, Best of 7
- **Team Sizes:** 1v1, 2v2, 5v5, 1v10
